About mAImona

mAImona is an experimental project demonstrating how live market data can be combined with AI to help traders make sense of the markets. It does not execute trades or offer financial advice.

Our mission is to provide clear, data-driven insights without the noise and misleading predictions that often accompany market analysis. We connect to real-time data sources and use AI to explain what's happening in the market in plain language.
